Fluffy Pancakes

‚è±Ô∏è 20 mins üçΩÔ∏è 2 servings üî• 350 cal/serving

Ingredients

  • 1 cup all-purpose flour
  • 2 tbsp sugar
  • 2 tsp baking powder
  • 1/2 tsp salt
  • 1 cup milk
  • 1 egg
  • 2 tbsp melted butter

Steps

  1. Mix dry ingredients in bowl
  2. Whisk wet ingredients separately
  3. Combine until just mixed (lumps are okay!)
  4. Heat pan over medium heat
  5. Pour 1/4 cup batter per pancake
  6. Flip when bubbles form (about 2-3 mins)
  7. Cook other side until golden
