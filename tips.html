Tips for Young Cooks

  • Always wash your hands before cooking.
  • Ask an adult to help with sharp tools and hot surfaces.
  • Read the recipe all the way through before starting.
  • Measure ingredients carefully to get the best results.
  • Clean up as you go to keep your space organized.
  • Be patient and take your time to learn.
  • Have fun and try new things!
